The Programming Language RTC   Program Design

Comments

    

In an RTC program, comments begin with the combination /* and end with */. Everything in between those two tokens is ignored by the compiler. Comments may extend over multiple program lines.

Alternatively, short comments may also be introduced by double backslash characters "\\". Those comments are terminated by the end of the line.

 

Statement_1;   /* Comment over...

                      ... multiple lines */

Statement_2;

Statement_3;   // Comment up to the end of line

Statement_4;

 

Comments are also accepted as delimiters between tokens.
